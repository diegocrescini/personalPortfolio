Vector

2018

Musically it feels like a logical step from 'Affinity', but lyrically it's a bit more theatrical and about as 'rock opera' as Haken has ever got. It's about a Doctor with an intriguing, perhaps sinister interest in a particular patient.

From there the story enters the point-of-view of the patient – who appears to be catatonic, but his mind is sparking with what could be memories, or delusions brought on by the treatment he's receiving – we leave this up to the listeners to decide. And that’s the challenge for the fans – to find out for themselves their own meaning for Vector as an album.
